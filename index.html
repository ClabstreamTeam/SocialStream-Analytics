FAQ

Frequently asked questions

How does annual billing work?

Annual plans are billed once per year and save 20% compared to monthly pricing. You retain the same feature set and gain long-term cost predictability.

What export formats are available?

Starter includes CSV export. Growth and Agency include PNG and PDF export for polished client reports and executive sharing.

How many seats do I get?

Starter includes one seat, Growth includes three seats, and Agency includes ten seats so your team can collaborate on analysis and reporting.

What does white-labeling include?

Agency-level white-labeling provides branded exports, client-ready dashboard assets, and presentation-ready reporting options for your team.

What is the Enterprise plan?

Enterprise is a custom offering starting at $999/mo with API access, SSO, custom deployment, dedicated onboarding, and higher usage limits.
